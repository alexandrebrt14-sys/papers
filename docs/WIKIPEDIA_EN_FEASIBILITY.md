## Applicable notability guidelines

### WP:GNG (General Notability Guideline)
Must have "significant coverage in reliable sources that are independent of the subject."

**Significant coverage = more than trivial mention, less than exclusive focus but enough to extract facts.**

### WP:NAUTHOR (author-specific)
Satisfies GNG OR at least one of:
1. Regarded as important figure or widely cited by peers
2. Known for originating a significant new concept, theory, or technique
3. Created significant or well-known work that has been subject of an independent book or multiple independent reviews
4. Work has won significant critical attention OR is a collective body

### WP:NACADEMIC (academic career)
Higher bar. Requires any of:
- Significant impact in academic discipline (citations, h-index)
- Highly prestigious academic award
- Named professorship or distinguished chair
- Member of National Academy equivalent
- Authored influential/standard textbook

### WP:NCREATIVE (practitioners)
Similar to NAUTHOR but for creators/innovators.
